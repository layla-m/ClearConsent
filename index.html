Why each partner needs access

☁️ AWS (Amazon Web Services) Stores your Vitalis data securely in encrypted servers. AWS cannot read your data, they only provide infrastructure. Covered by a strict data processing agreement.
📈 Mixpanel (Analytics) Receives anonymised usage events (e.g. "user viewed dashboard"). Your name and health data are never shared. You can opt out in Your Choices.
📬 SendGrid (Email) Delivers your Vitalis health digest and account notifications. Only your email address is passed. They cannot access your health records.
✓ What Vitalis never does We do not sell your data, share it with insurers or employers, or use it for targeted advertising on other platforms.

How long Vitalis stores each data type

⚠ What happens when you delete your Vitalis account? All personal data is permanently erased within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated statistics may be retained for health research but cannot be traced back to you.

Retention periods comply with Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA). Request earlier deletion at privacy@vitalis.health.

🔒 What is ClearConsent™?

ClearConsent™ is an independent, interactive privacy consent platform embedded by Vitalis during the sign-up process.

Why does Vitalis use it?

Traditional privacy policies are long, dense, and difficult to understand. ClearConsent replaces the standard "wall of text" with a structured, step-by-step review that explains, in plain language, exactly what data is collected, who can see it, how long it is kept, and what your rights are.

What does ClearConsent do?

It walks you through five sections: what Vitalis collects, who the data is shared with, how long it is retained, your legal rights under PIPEDA, and your ability to customise your consent preferences.

Is my data shared with ClearConsent?

ClearConsent only records a timestamped log of your consent choices, it does not receive or store your personal health data. Your health information stays with Vitalis.

Can I change my preferences later?

Yes. You can review and update your consent choices at any time from Vitalis > Settings > Privacy.
